Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection.

Preparation time

  • Total: 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter or oil

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Heat the pan
  3. Cook the omelette
  4. Add fillings
  5. Fold and serve

Nutrition

Calories 277 kcal
Protein 20g
Fat 22g
